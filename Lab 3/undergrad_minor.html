Undergraduate Minor

The ITWS minor requires a minimum of 16 credit hours that must be approved by the minor advisor in ITWS.

Requirements

  • ITWS-1100 Introduction to ITWS
  • ITWS-4310 Managing IT Resources
  • Humanities Elective (one of):
    • ITWS-1220 IT and Society
    • COMM-4790 Social Impact of Electronic Media
  • Technical Elective (one of):
    • ITWS-2110 Web Systems Development
    • CSCI-2300 Introduction to Algorithms
    • CSCI-4380 Database Systems
    • MGMT-4170 Data Resource Management
