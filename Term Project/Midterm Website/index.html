A web-based application

Designed to enhance the personal timekeeping and organisation of its users

CommuniTea is designed specifically for communities such as Universities - by displaying live feeds from information media such as Newsletters, Club Events, and Instagram Promotions, CommuniTea allows users to stay up to date with local activities in their Community.

By combining commonly-used tools such as calendars, reminders, and news bulletins, CommuniTea allows users to stay up to date with their plans.

Timekeeping and Organisation

CommuniTea is formatted in a user-friendly, visually-appealing way, in which a monthly calendar displays time-blocked events, as well as task reminders.
